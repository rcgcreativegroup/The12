What Is The 12

One City.
Infinite Voices.

The 12 is Memphis's first premier vocal competition series — an 8-episode TV experience open to singers of every genre. R&B. Gospel. Country. Hip-Hop. Pop. Soul. If you have a voice and a story, you belong here.

12 vocalists are selected from open auditions. They compete across performances, challenges, and live events — with audiences voting, judges guiding, and Memphis watching.

This isn't just a competition. It's a platform, a launchpad, and a celebration of the talent that's always been in this city.

The Show

8 Episodes.
One Winner.

EP 1 – 2 Open Auditions Singers of every genre compete for 12 coveted spots. Stories are told. Voices are heard. Memphis takes notice.
EP 3 – 4 Bootcamp Coaching, cross-genre challenges, and eliminations. Artists are pushed beyond what they thought was possible.
EP 5 – 6 The Knockouts Head-to-head battles. Fan voting opens. Every genre — and every artist — has a real shot.
EP 7 Semi-Final Top 4 face off live. Audience vote meets judge decision. Stakes have never been higher.
EP 8 The Finale Live ticketed event. Memphis votes. One voice wins it all and carries this city's story to the world.

How to Enter

Your Shot.
